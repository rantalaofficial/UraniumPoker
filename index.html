How to Play Uranium Poker

Click 'Deal' to start a new game. Adjust your bet with arrows. Click cards to LOCK them, then click Draw to replace the rest.

After a win, click Double to gamble winnings against the dealer. A pair always beats a high card, and a higher pair beats a lower pair. If both have high cards, the highest card wins. If there's a tie, the dealer wins.

Uranium: The meter fills with each losing hand. When it reaches 100%, a meltdown occurs on your next deal, guaranteeing a hand with only face cards (J, Q, K, A)!
